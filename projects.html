Projects & Case Studies

A selection of infrastructure, environmental, and engineering projects successfully supported and delivered by LG Supply Ltd across Southern Africa.

Lesotho Lowlands Water Development Project – Phase II

Sector: Water Infrastructure Region: Southern Africa Project Type: Bulk Water Supply Systems

Project Overview

LG Supply Ltd supported a large-scale national water infrastructure programme aimed at improving domestic, industrial, and institutional water supply in rapidly growing urban and peri-urban areas.

Scope of Work

The project involved technical review of engineering designs, support for design-and-build procurement processes, preparation of tender documentation, and construction supervision of intake works, treatment facilities, transmission pipelines, and distribution networks.

Key Outcomes

The assignment contributed to improved water supply reliability, strengthened project governance, and ensured compliance with international engineering standards while supporting long-term operational sustainability.

Environmental Flow Assessment & Water Quality Modelling

Sector: Environmental & Water Resources Region: Southern Africa Project Type: Environmental Assessment

Project Overview

LG Supply Ltd delivered specialist environmental and hydrological services to support a strategic water abstraction and augmentation initiative while safeguarding river ecosystems.

Scope of Work

Services included environmental flow assessments, hydrological and hydraulic analysis, aquatic biodiversity studies, water quality modelling, and the development of sustainable flow management frameworks.

Key Outcomes

The project established clear environmental flow requirements, strengthened environmental compliance, and provided practical operational guidelines balancing infrastructure needs with ecosystem protection.

Multi-Phase Water Infrastructure Planning & Supervision

Sector: Infrastructure Development Region: Southern Africa Project Type: Planning & Supervision

Project Overview

LG Supply Ltd supported a multi-phase water infrastructure development program designed to expand and modernize regional water supply systems serving growing urban centers.

Scope of Work

The scope covered design reviews, tender preparation, procurement support, contractor evaluation, and construction supervision across treatment plants, reservoirs, pumping stations, and distribution networks.

Key Outcomes

The project achieved improved delivery coordination, enhanced construction quality, and increased system capacity and resilience.
