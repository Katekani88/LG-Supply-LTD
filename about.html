About LG Supply Ltd

LG Supply Ltd is a buyer-led procurement authority focused on sourcing engineering equipment for high-demand sectors across Africa, ensuring transparent, compliant, and efficient tendering.

Our Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To be Africa's leading procurement aggregator, providing ethical and efficient tendering while connecting suppliers with high-value infrastructure projects.

Our Vision

To streamline procurement for critical infrastructure, creating transparency, fostering supplier growth, and driving regional development.

Our Role in the Value Chain

Identify Needs

We analyze client projects and market requirements for procurement.

Issue Tenders

Transparent tendering using regional and international standards.

Aggregate Supplier Bids

We evaluate supplier submissions ethically and effectively.

Coordinate Fulfilment

Seamless integration with LG Supply for end-to-end procurement delivery.

UK-Tanzania Advantage

LG Supply Ltd leverages UK advisory expertise for international sourcing standards while maintaining a strong local presence in Africa, ensuring compliance, quality, and market responsiveness for all procurement projects.
