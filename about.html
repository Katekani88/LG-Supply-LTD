About LG Supply Ltd

LG Supply Ltd is a buyer-led procurement authority focused on sourcing engineering equipment for high-demand sectors across Africa, ensuring transparent, compliant, and efficient tendering.

Our Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To be Africa's leading procurement aggregator, providing ethical and efficient tendering while connecting suppliers with high-value infrastructure projects. We strive to reduce procurement risks and enhance operational efficiency for all stakeholders.

Our Vision

To streamline procurement for critical infrastructure, creating transparency, fostering supplier growth, and driving regional development. Our goal is to enable sustainable economic growth by linking demand and supply efficiently.

Our Role in the Value Chain

Identify Needs

We analyze client projects and market requirements for procurement, providing insights to optimize sourcing strategies and reduce inefficiencies.

Issue Tenders

Transparent tendering using regional and international standards ensures compliance and fair market access for all suppliers.

Aggregate Supplier Bids

We evaluate supplier submissions independently, ensuring ethical and efficient decision-making while maintaining market neutrality.

Coordinate Fulfilment

Seamless integration with LG Supply for end-to-end procurement delivery, monitoring project milestones and supplier performance for optimal results.

UK-Tanzania Advantage

LG Supply Ltd leverages UK advisory expertise for international sourcing standards while maintaining a strong local presence in Africa. This ensures compliance, quality, and market responsiveness for all procurement projects, delivering measurable economic value and financial efficiency for our clients.

Our cross-border collaboration brings best practices in risk management, supplier governance, and market insights, enabling institutions to access resources strategically and responsibly.
