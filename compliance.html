Compliance & Standards

LG Supply ensures all tenders meet Tanzanian Bureau of Standards (TBS) regulations, PVoC routes, and ethical procurement guidelines.

TBS & PVoC Compliance

Route A

For occasional shipments: inspection and testing required before customs clearance.

Route B

For registered products: frequent exporters submit certificates of conformity (CoC) via accredited bodies like Intertek.

Route C

High-volume licensed exporters: factory audits and full compliance checks mandatory.

Supplier Requirements

Certificates

Bidders must provide CoC, test reports, and certificates of origin for each consignment.

Local Content

Suppliers are encouraged to comply with EAC and Tanzanian local content preferences.

Ethical Standards

All tenders enforce anti-corruption and ethical bidding practices to maintain transparency.
