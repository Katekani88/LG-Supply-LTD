Anti-Corruption Policy

Zero tolerance for bribery, fraud, and unethical conduct

1. Policy Statement

LG Supply Ltd is committed to conducting business with integrity, transparency, and accountability. We maintain a strict zero-tolerance approach to corruption, bribery, and unethical practices.

2. Scope

This policy applies to all employees, directors, agents, suppliers, contractors, and partners engaged with DemandCo Ltd.

3. Prohibited Conduct

  • Offering or accepting bribes or kickbacks
  • Improper payments to public officials
  • Facilitation payments of any kind
  • False documentation or misrepresentation

4. Supplier & Partner Expectations

All suppliers and partners must comply with applicable anti-corruption laws, including South African, Tanzanian, UK, and EAC regulations.

5. Reporting Violations

Any suspected or actual violations must be reported immediately through official communication channels. Reports will be handled confidentially.

6. Enforcement

Breaches of this policy may result in termination of contracts, legal action, or reporting to relevant authorities.
